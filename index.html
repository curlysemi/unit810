Life at Orpheum Lofts: Walkable, Historic, and Uniquely Downtown

This Art Deco high-rise blends vintage character with modern urban living, placing you just steps from restaurants, theaters, museums, light rail, and major sports venues.

The unit features true loft-style design with exposed brick walls, concrete beam ceilings, and wood flooring in the main living area. The open layout offers a bright, flexible space for living and dining, complemented by ceiling fans and clean architectural lines. The kitchen includes European cabinetry, black granite tile, and ceramic flooring, while the bathroom features maple cabinetry and a tile backsplash for a clean, modern feel.

Residents enjoy access to a full suite of amenities, including a heated pool and spa, fitness center, clubhouse, and BBQ area, all within a secure, professionally maintained building.

Originally built in 1931 and listed on the National Register of Historic Places, the building also has a unique place in pop culture—featured in the opening scene of Psycho.

If you're looking for a downtown lifestyle with character, walkability, and skyline views, this is it.
